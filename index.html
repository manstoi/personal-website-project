The Educated Psychotherapist


Helping bridge the research-implementation gap in mental health

The Educated Psychotherapist is a platform dedicated to providing evidence-based resources and information for mental health professionals. Our mission is to help bridge the gap between research and practice, ensuring that therapists have access to the latest findings in the field.

Our Goals:

  • To provide high-quality, evidence-based resources for mental health professionals.
  • To promote the integration of research into clinical practice.
  • To foster a community of informed and educated therapists.

Our Services:

  • Online courses and workshops on evidence-based practices.
  • Access to a library of research articles and resources.
  • Networking opportunities with other mental health professionals.

In addition to our resources, we also offer a blog that covers a wide range of topics related to mental health, therapy, and research. Our goal is to create a space where therapists can come together to learn, share, and grow in their practice. For more information about our services, please feel free to reach out to us through our contact page. We look forward to hearing from you and helping you on your journey as a mental health professional. Additionally, don't forget to subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates, resources, and exclusive content tailored for mental health professionals. Together, we can make a difference in the field of mental health.
