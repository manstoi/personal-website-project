The Psychotherapist: Manny Stoilov


Manny Stoilov is a licensed psychotherapist with over 10 years of experience in the field. He has a passion for helping individuals and families navigate their mental health challenges. Manny's approach to therapy is rooted in empathy, understanding, and evidence-based practices. He believes in the power of the therapeutic relationship and works collaboratively with his clients to achieve their goals.

Manny has a diverse background in various therapeutic modalities, including cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), and mindfulness-based approaches. He is dedicated to staying current with the latest research and techniques in the field to provide the best possible care for his clients.

In addition to his clinical work, Manny is also a sought-after speaker and educator. He has presented at numerous conferences and workshops, sharing his expertise on topics such as trauma-informed care, self-care for therapists, and the importance of cultural competence in therapy. Manny is committed to promoting mental health awareness and reducing the stigma surrounding mental illness.

Outside of his professional life, Manny enjoys spending time with his family, hiking, and practicing mindfulness. He believes in the importance of self-care and strives to maintain a healthy work-life balance. Manny is passionate about making a positive impact in the lives of others and is dedicated to helping individuals achieve their full potential.
